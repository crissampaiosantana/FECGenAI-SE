# Questionnaire

This directory contains the survey instrument used to collect data for the study.

## Contents

- **Survey_Portuguese.pdf** – Original questionnaire administered to Software Engineering professionals.
- **Survey_English.pdf** – English version of the questionnaire (if available).

## Description

The questionnaire was designed to evaluate the influence of cultural values, Artificial Intelligence Literacy, trust, risk perception, cognitive mediation, and technology adoption factors on the adoption of Generative Artificial Intelligence in Software Engineering.

The instrument was developed based on validated constructs adapted from previous studies, including Cultural Values Scale (CVSCALE), Technology Acceptance Model (TAM), Unified Theory of Acceptance and Use of Technology (UTAUT/UTAUT2), and Artificial Intelligence Literacy literature.

The questionnaire was administered online to Software Engineering professionals.
